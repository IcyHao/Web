Studying Spaces

Anytime you walk into the Student's Union building you will be able to find the perfect space to study, there are a few examples below:

Support Services

Student Services Information Desk

This is the first place to go if you have any problems or questions regarding the university. Personally I think this is the heart of the Students' Union. You could ask for a "proof of student status" paper if you need one or if you lost your UCard, you can get a new one here, or literally whatever your problem is they can help you here, so make sure you remember this place!

Student Support & Guidance

Are you an international student with problems, or having financial hardship? The Student Support & Guidance desk is the place to go. Remember, it's not an embarassing thing to have problems, everybody has problems, the most important thing is to make sure people know about your problems and if not, feel free to ask for some help!

Retail Outlets

Our Shop

You can find all kind of food including international food, sandwiches, coffee, snacks, drinks, stationery ,and a lot more in Our Shop which is located right in the middle of the ground floor.

Sheffield Students' Union Store

If you want to buy one of them cool "University of Sheffield" signed jumpers, this is the place you are looking for! They are selling clothes, accessories, stationery and gifts.

Box Office

The box office is the centre of the students welfare. You can get tickets for the SU events, including club nights, karaoke, cinema, and many more. If you are interested in the sport events, you can also purchase tickets for the matches here. The box office is located on Level 3, just opposite the main entrance.

Cash Machines

These cach machines are located just next to the main entrance, if you ever run low on cash it's handy to know you can get cash easily around the SU.
